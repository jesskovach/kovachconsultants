About KCG

Built from the belief that leadership can repair what systems have fractured.

I founded Kovach Consulting Group after more than two decades of leading through mergers, acquisitions, crises, and cultural transformation — and witnessing firsthand the cost of systems that measure performance but don't hear people.

At KCG, we work at the intersection of leadership development, organizational design, and human-centered strategy — helping leaders realign purpose with practice and build organizations that are transparent, regenerative, and equitable.

Every framework, tool, and engagement is guided by the same principle that started this: integrity is strategy.

"Fix the system, and people can breathe again."

How we work

We start by listening to what the system is already saying.

Most organizational problems aren't new. They're patterns that haven't been named yet. I start with sense-making — understanding what's actually happening before prescribing what should change. From there, we diagnose, design, and integrate alongside the people doing the work.

"This isn't a performance issue — it's a trust issue. Let's name that so we can fix it."
