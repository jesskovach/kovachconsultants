About KCG

Built from the belief that leadership can repair what systems have fractured.

Kovach Consulting Group was founded after more than two decades of leading through mergers, acquisitions, crises, and cultural transformation — and witnessing firsthand the cost of systems that measure performance but don't hear people.

Leadership development, organizational design, and human-centered strategy — brought together in service of leaders who are ready to close the gap between what their organizations say and what they actually do.

This work is grounded in a few things I keep coming back to.

What I believe

  • Clarity is a form of care.
  • Most people aren't struggling because they lack skill — they're navigating systems not designed for them.
  • Naming what's actually happening is the first step toward restoring agency.
  • Structural harm is too often misnamed as personal failure.
  • Integrity is strategy.

How we work

We start by listening to what the system is already saying.

Most organizational problems aren't new. They're patterns that haven't been named yet. I start with sense-making — understanding what's actually happening before prescribing what should change. From there, we diagnose, design, and integrate alongside the people doing the work.

"This isn't a performance issue — it's a trust issue. Let's name that so we can fix it."
