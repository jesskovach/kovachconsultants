Kovach Consulting Group is a limited liability company (LLC) operated by Jess Kovach. This page explains what information is collected when you use this site and how it is handled.

Information collected

This site does not use tracking cookies, analytics software, or advertising networks. No personal data is collected passively by visiting this site.

If you submit a form on this site — through the contact page or the writing page — your submission is processed by Formspree and delivered to privacy@kovachconsultants.com. Formspree's privacy policy governs how form data is handled on their end. KCG does not store form submissions independently.

If you schedule a conversation through Calendly, that interaction is governed by Calendly's privacy policy.

How information is used

Information submitted through forms is used only to respond to your inquiry. It is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

Email

If you contact KCG directly by email, your message and contact information are retained only as long as necessary to respond and follow up. You can request deletion of your information at any time by emailing privacy@kovachconsultants.com.

Third-party services

This site links to external platforms including Substack and LinkedIn. Visiting those platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies. KCG has no control over data collected by those services.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The current version is always available at kovachconsultants.com/privacy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@kovachconsultants.com.
