## Common Startup Questions

### "Should I read the full PMBOK first?"
**Answer**: No. Read chapters 1-4 (foundations), then jump to studying via your projects. PMBOK is 650 pages; reading all before practicing = inefficient. Your projects teach better than reading.

### "Can I skip the design system?"
**Answer**: Yes, but don't. It's the least "required" tool, but it:
- Saves time on every project (reuse vs. rebuild)
- Looks professional to clients
- Generates consulting credibility
- Teaches Quality knowledge area

Minimum: Document 5 components you use.

### "What if I don't have projects yet?"
**Answer**: Use hypothetical projects from PMBOK examples, or build one small feature and apply the framework to it. The point is *practicing thinking*, not perfect projects.

### "When do I take the PMP exam?"
**Answer**: After 12 weeks of this system, you'll be ready. Book it now (4-month window) so you have a deadline. PMI registration: $1,000 exam + $555 membership (first year).

### "Should I buy expensive PMP prep course?"
**Answer**: No. Spend instead:
- PMBOK book: $70-100
- Udemy course: $15
- This system: Free
- Total: ~$100

More expensive bootcamps ($2,000+) aren't better; you learn better from real projects anyway.
