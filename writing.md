This is where the work goes public.

I write and speak about leadership under pressure, organizational systems, ethical decision-making, and what it takes to build structures that actually protect the people inside them. The through line is sense-making — naming what's happening before prescribing what should change.

### What I cover

**Leadership under pressure** — What happens to decision-making, communication, and accountability when the stakes are high and the structure isn't holding. Pattern recognition from the inside, not theory from a distance.

**Organizational systems** — Why capable people fail in broken systems, and what it takes to fix the structure rather than blame the person.

**Ethical decision-making** — How leaders navigate the gap between stated values and actual practice — and what it costs when they don't.

**Institutional accountability** — Who gets held responsible, how accountability gets distributed (and avoided), and what transparent leadership looks like in practice.

**Building structures that protect people** — Compliance, operations, and design choices that make organizations safer and more sustainable for everyone inside them.

## Speaking & Facilitation

I speak at leadership convenings, organizational offsites, and public forums on the same themes I write about — with an emphasis on practical application over abstract frameworks.

Talks and workshops are designed around your audience's specific context. I don't deliver the same keynote twice.

**Topics include:**
- Leadership under pressure and in transition
- Sense-making and organizational diagnosis
- Ethical decision-making and institutional accountability
- Building equitable, transparent systems
- Neurodivergent and LGBTQIA+ leadership in institutional contexts
